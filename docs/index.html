Overview

This dashboard was developed to monitor progress with the training of service delivery points (SDPs) which includes mapped health facilities, TBAs, Faith, Birth Homes and SDPs newly identified in the 774 LGAs across the country. It displays the performance at the national, state and at LGA levels.

The states are grouped by geo-political zone and can be assessed by a click on the appropriate tab. Each state is allocated a row with its data distributed across three tabs. The names and contents of the tabs are outlined below.

  • State/LGA Map: Chloropeth map displaying LGAs progress within each state with mapping of total service delivery points including the mapped facilities and the new SDPs.

  • Percentage of total service delivery points trained: Doughnut charts showing the percentage of total service delivery points in the mapped and trained, newly identified and mapped and not yet mapped in each local government area of the state.

  • Number of service delivery points trained by State: Table with five columns with self-explanatory headings.

The data displayed on this dashboard is collected and reported during the training of the SDPs by the local government teams using a standardized data collection tool on their smart phones. It is updated in the morning (0700-1000).

For further information, please contact the National Coordinator, NASCP. Email: nga.nascp@gmail.com
